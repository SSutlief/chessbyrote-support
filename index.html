ChessByRote

A chess opening trainer for iOS.

About

ChessByRote helps you memorize chess opening move sequences through repetitive practice. You play one side, the app plays the other, and you drill openings until they're second nature. You can also create your own openings and add move-by-move commentary. ChessByRote does not support general chess play.

Support

For questions, bug reports, or feedback, please email chessbyrote@gmail.com.

Privacy

ChessByRote does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All practice statistics and custom openings are stored locally on your device. The app does not connect to the internet.
